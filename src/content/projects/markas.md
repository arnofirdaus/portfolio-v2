MARKAS is a centralized administrative platform that enables local Education Authorities to monitor, verify, and approve school budget proposals (RKAS) across Indonesia. Working hand-in-hand with ARKAS, this platform ensures efficient data synchronization, transparent fund oversight, and secure, role-based control for managing regional educational finances.
